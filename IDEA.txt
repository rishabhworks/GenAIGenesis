TradePass – AI Career Agent for Skilled Trades Workers

Project Idea

TradePass is a voice-first AI career assistant designed for skilled
trades workers who may not have resumes, LinkedIn profiles, or strong
digital literacy. The platform allows workers to describe their
experience using voice and automatically generates a structured
professional profile. Using this profile, the system finds job
opportunities, detects underpaid positions, and explains complex
contracts in simple language.

This solution addresses a major labor gap: Canada faces a large shortage
of skilled trades workers, yet many qualified workers struggle to access
opportunities due to digital barriers. TradePass bridges this gap by
providing an accessible, AI-powered job assistant that works entirely
through voice and simple interactions.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

Core Features

1.  Voice-Based Worker Profile Creation Workers speak about their
    experience. AI converts the speech into a structured professional
    profile including trade, experience, certifications, specialties,
    and location.

2.  Intelligent Job Matching The system finds nearby job opportunities
    that match the worker’s skills and experience.

3.  Pay Fairness Detection The system analyzes job pay rates against
    regional market wage data and alerts workers if a job is
    significantly underpaying.

4.  Plain Language Contract Explanation Workers can upload or paste job
    contract clauses, and the AI explains them in simple language so
    workers understand their rights.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

System Workflow Architecture

Step 1: Voice Input The worker records a short voice description of
their work experience.

Step 2: Speech-to-Text Processing The audio is converted into text using
an AI speech recognition service.

Step 3: AI Skill Extraction An AI model analyzes the transcript and
extracts structured information such as: - Trade - Years of experience -
Certifications - Specialties - Location

Step 4: Worker Profile Creation The extracted data is saved as a digital
worker profile in the database.

Step 5: Job Data Collection The system gathers job postings from online
job boards and contractor listings.

Step 6: Job Matching Engine The AI compares job requirements with the
worker profile and generates a list of relevant job matches.

Step 7: Pay Fairness Analysis The system compares job wages with
regional trade wage data. If a job pays significantly below the market
rate, the system flags it and alerts the worker.

Step 8: Contract Explanation Workers can submit job contract text. The
AI translates the legal language into simple, understandable
explanations.

Impact

TradePass improves access to employment opportunities for skilled trades
workers by: - Removing the need for resumes - Providing wage
transparency - Helping workers understand employment contracts -
Connecting qualified workers with relevant jobs

The platform improves productivity in the labor market while also
delivering social impact by empowering underserved workers.
